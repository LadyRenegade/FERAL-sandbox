FERAL Safety Features
Crisis Detection — Non-Negotiable

Crisis detection triggers anywhere in the app — not just the community section
If someone is in crisis it does not matter what section they are in
This gets built first — it is not a Version 2 feature

Crisis Detection Trigger
When certain words or phrases related to suicide or self harm appear anywhere in the app a crisis popup appears automatically.
The popup message:
Hey. We see you. If you're struggling right now please reach out to the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline. Call or text 988. You don't have to figure this out alone. 🌿
988 is free, confidential, and available 24/7 by call or text.
Community Moderation

Any comment in the community section that triggers crisis detection also gets flagged for human review
In the beginning that person is Jen
Eventually a small team of trusted moderators
Admin has hidden session ID access to identify repeat bad actors
Bans are final with no appeals process
