I was on fiber supplements and all I ate were fruits and vegetables. I still couldn't shit. Sometimes for two weeks. And the pills that did help weren't covered by my insurance.
Then my stomach gave out.
Now what the fuck am I supposed to do?
It didn't work.

Awesome. I say that dripping with disdain. I guess the fact that I was LITERALLY STARVING BECAUSE I COULDN'T KEEP FOOD DOWN DOESN'T MATTER AND IT'S POINTLESS TO TRY TO HELP ME?! COOL!
I left that appointment sobbing. I'm a giant cry baby but I still feel it was called for. I was so defeated. I honestly thought I was going to die. I'm tearing up writing this.

No real answers. Just the same advice I've been hearing my entire life that doesn't work. And no one listened. I was begging for answers into the void.
You are not alone in your fight to be heard.

And if you know me you know how hard it is for me to stay focused that long! I was so mad about the entire situation it was a breeze.)
