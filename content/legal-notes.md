FERAL Legal Notes and Intentions
These are not finished legal documents. This is Jen's intentions written down so a lawyer can turn them into real legal language someday.
What FERAL Will Never Do
FERAL will never sell user data. Ever.
FERAL will never share personal information with third parties.
FERAL will never store sensitive user information on its end.
FERAL will never make medical claims.
FERAL will never pretend to be a crisis platform.
What FERAL Commits To
FERAL will follow HIPAA principles even though it is not legally required to.
FERAL will be transparent about exactly what it does and does not collect.
FERAL will give users control over their own information.
FERAL will store nothing it does not absolutely need.
Age and Content
This app contains adult language and mildly inappropriate content. No, not THAT kind. Calm down.
Users must confirm they are 18 or older, or have parental consent before accessing the app.
What FERAL Is Not
FERAL is not a medical platform.
FERAL is not a crisis service.
FERAL is not a substitute for professional help.
FERAL is not a therapy app.
